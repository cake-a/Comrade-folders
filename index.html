Welcome, Comrade.

Our mission is to prepare students for higher education,

and ensure higher success rates for students taking the financial risk towards learning.


Our hypothesis is,

if students share their pathways, booklists, and reading guides for their major & school,

then they can increase success rates for future students by allowing them to prepare prior to entering courses.


Life is not a competition, but teamwork. Our common goal is maximum efficiency in all facets of life, and peace.

Let us be comrades together toward greater success rates in higher education, so nobody gets left behind.
